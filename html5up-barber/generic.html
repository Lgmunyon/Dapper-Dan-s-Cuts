Our Story

Our Story So Far...

Our founders grew up in Forest Park and went into the military. After returning to their community they noticed a lack of proper, quality mens' barbershops and thus,the first Dapper Dan's Cuts was opened. Since then, they have been lucky enough to open a second location in Buckhead. Both locations are now staffed with trained professionals from New York, Chicago, LA, and of course Atlana.
